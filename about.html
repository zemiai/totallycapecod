About Totally Cape Cod

A locals-first guide to the best beaches, eats, stays, and events from Bourne to Provincetown — kept fresh every day.

Why we built this

Cape Cod has hundreds of beaches, restaurants, and things to do, but the info is scattered across town websites, old PDFs, and word of mouth. We wanted one beautiful, fast place that answers the question every visitor actually asks: "What should I do today?"

What's inside

  • Live conditions — beach status, water temps, tides, and bridge traffic so you can plan around the crowds.
  • Curated picks — the beaches, lobster rolls, sunset spots, and hidden gems worth your time.
  • Daily events — concerts, trivia nights, markets, and drive-in movies across all 15 Cape towns.
  • A free app — add it to your home screen and check the Cape from your pocket.

How we stay free

Totally Cape Cod is free to use. We keep the lights on through affiliate links (when you book a hotel or tour) and paid business listings. Those never change our honest recommendations — see our Privacy Policy and Terms for the details.
